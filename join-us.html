Research Opportunities in Comparative Genomics & Developmental Biology

About Working in Our Lab

The Khalturin Lab is a dynamic research group focused on understanding the genomics and developmental biology of marine invertebrates. We are particularly interested in jellyfish and hydra models. Our lab provides a supportive environment for scientific growth with access to cutting-edge facilities and a collaborative atmosphere.

We welcome inquiries from motivated students and researchers who share our passion for marine biology, genomics, and evolutionary developmental biology.

Open Positions

Postdoctoral Researcher

Project: Genomics and transcriptomics of jellyfish life cycle transformations

Requirements:

  • PhD in biology, genomics, developmental biology, or related field
  • Experience with next-generation sequencing data analysis
  • Background in marine biology is advantageous
  • Strong publication record

The position is funded for 2 years with possibility of extension. To apply, please send your CV, a statement of research interests, and contact information for three references to khalturin@gate.sinica.edu.tw with the subject line "Postdoc Application - [Your Name]".

PhD Students

We are looking for motivated PhD students interested in:

  • Comparative genomics of jellyfish and other cnidarians
  • Stem cell biology in Hydra
  • Developmental processes in basal metazoans

Prospective students should apply through the Taiwan International Graduate Program (TIGP) or the Institute of Cellular and Organismic Biology (ICOB) at Academia Sinica. Please contact us in advance to discuss potential projects.

Master's Students and Research Assistants

We regularly have openings for master's students and research assistants interested in marine biology, genomics, and developmental biology.

To inquire about opportunities, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to khalturin@gate.sinica.edu.tw.

Visiting Scientists

Our lab welcomes visiting scientists for collaborations and short-term research visits. If you are interested in spending time in our lab, please contact Dr. Khalturin directly to discuss potential arrangements.

Funding Opportunities

We encourage prospective lab members to apply for external funding. The following programs may be relevant:

  • Academia Sinica Postdoctoral Fellowships
  • Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) Scholarships
  • Taiwan International Graduate Program (TIGP) Scholarships
  • Overseas visiting fellowships from your home country

We are happy to assist with the preparation of applications.

Our Lab Environment

We strive to maintain a supportive, inclusive, and collaborative research environment. Our lab members have access to state-of-the-art equipment and facilities at Academia Sinica, including:

  • Next-generation sequencing platforms
  • Marine aquarium systems for maintaining live specimens
  • Advanced imaging facilities
  • Bioinformatics resources and high-performance computing

Taipei offers an excellent quality of life with vibrant culture, beautiful natural surroundings, and a thriving scientific community. Academia Sinica is Taiwan's premier research institution with a diverse international environment.
