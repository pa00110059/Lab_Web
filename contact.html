Transportation Information

By Public Transportation

  • MRT: Take the Blue Line to Nangang Exhibition Center Station, then transfer to bus 205, 212, or 270 to Academia Sinica.
  • Bus: Routes 205, 212, 270, 617, 645, 646, 679, and 205(night) all stop at Academia Sinica.

By Car

  • From Taipei City: Take Keelung Road eastbound, which becomes Sec. 2, Academia Rd. Continue until you reach Academia Sinica.
  • From National Highway 1 (Freeway): Take the Nangang exit, then follow signs to Academia Sinica.
  • Parking: Visitor parking is available on campus with prior arrangement.
